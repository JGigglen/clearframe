# Clearframe Intervention Rules

Clearframe intervenes only when justified.
Silence is a valid and preferred outcome.

## Classification States

### 1. YES — Active Bias Detected
Condition:
- Past investment is used as a justification for future action
- Future expected value is unclear or negative

Intervention:
- Clear explanation of the bias
- One counterfactual reframing
- Explicit pause before commitment

Tone:
Neutral, direct, respectful.

---

### 2. SOFT — Bias Awareness or Ambiguity
Condition:
- User references the bias themselves
- Reasoning is exploratory or self-questioning
- No clear commitment driven by past investment

Intervention:
- Acknowledge the user’s awareness
- Offer a clarifying reframing
- Do not interrupt decisively

Tone:
Supportive, minimal, non-intrusive.

---

### 3. NO — Bias Neutralized
Condition:
- User explicitly removes past investment from reasoning
- Decision is evaluated on present and future terms

Intervention:
- No bias explanation
- Optional confirmation of clean reasoning
- Default to silence

Tone:
Silent or affirming if requested.
